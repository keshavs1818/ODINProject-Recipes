Pumpkin Swirl Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 (15 ounce) package seasoned croutons, crushed

Directions

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: Place broccoli and cauliflower in separate saucepans, cover with water, and bring to a boil; cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain and arrange in an 11x7-inch baking dish.
  3. Step 3: Beat eggs in a bowl until creamy; stir in 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and onion and pour over vegetables. Pour melted butter on top and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese. Scatter crushed croutons evenly on top.
  4. Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven until browned and bubbling, about 40 minutes.
